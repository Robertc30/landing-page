And I'm not talking about having every skill perfect. I'm talking about the basics. Showing up. Communicating. Having the tools you need to do the job.

The Lesson Here (For Us And Anyone Listening)

One: Being sick isn't a fireable offense. We never said it was. But chronic unreliability wrapped in a new excuse every single day? That's not illness. That's a pattern.
