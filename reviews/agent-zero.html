Agent Zero

The framework for fully autonomous AI agents.

The Verdict

Agent Zero represents a shift from "chatbots" to "operators." It is designed to inhabit a shell, execute code, and solve complex problems without constant hand-holding.

Why it's in the stack

High agency. Low friction. It allows for the kind of "set it and forget it" automation that TechQuest.AI is built on.
