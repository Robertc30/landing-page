The Story Behind It

I personally know the guy behind this product, and I was there when the idea for it clicked. He had just moved into a new place, and I was helping him bring a couch up the stairs. I said it would fit. He said it wouldn't.

After 30 minutes of struggling, we gave up. It might have worked (if I was a little taller and my arms weren't jelly after endless curls at the gym)… but we'll never know.

Most of us have been in some version of that situation. That moment is what led him to build ItemFits — an app that helps you answer those questions before you buy or move something. Will that couch fit? Will a king-size bed work in your room? What size truck do you actually need for a move?

This app is designed to take the guesswork out of all of it.

What It Does

The web experience itself is clean, simple, and thoughtfully designed. It's still a work in progress, but it already solves a very real, very common problem:

If you've ever wrestled furniture through a doorway or made a bad guess at IKEA, it's worth checking out. Enter your dimensions, enter the item dimensions, and get an answer instead of a gamble.

Verdict

ItemFits attacks a universal pain point with simplicity. No accounts, no upsells, just a calculator that answers the question before you regret not asking it. The fact that it's built by someone who lived the problem shows in the thoughtful design choices.

Recommended if you've ever underestimated a doorway or overestimated a trunk.
