NotebookLM

AI-powered research assistant that talks to your documents

Reviewed February 3, 2026 · Use case: Research & AI

NotebookLM is Google's AI-powered research tool. Upload documents, and Gemini will answer questions, summarize content, and — most uniquely — generate audio summaries that sound like a podcast hosts discussing your material.

How I Use It

Rob uploads PDFs, articles, and notes to get AI-powered summaries and insights. The Audio Overview feature turns static documents into conversational audio — surprisingly useful for absorbing content while multitasking.

Pros

  • Ground truth in your sources (no hallucination)
  • Audio Overview is genuinely useful
  • Supports multiple document formats
  • Free tier with generous limits
  • Seamless Google ecosystem integration

Cons

  • Limited to uploaded documents (no web search)
  • Audio generation can be slow
  • Requires Google account

Verdict

The best tool for "I have a pile of documents, help me understand them." The grounded responses mean it won't make things up — a rarity in AI tools.
