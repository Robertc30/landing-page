Gemini

Google's multimodal AI model with a surprisingly capable free tier

Reviewed February 3, 2026 · Use case: AI & Productivity

Gemini is Google's flagship multimodal AI model. It handles text, code, images, and more — with a free tier that punches well above its weight class. The 2.0 Flash models are fast, capable, and free.

How I Use It

Gemini serves as a quick-check model for Rob — fast queries, image analysis, and backup when MiniMax needs a breather. The free tier means zero cost for everyday tasks.

Pros

  • Generous free tier (1,500 requests/day)
  • Fast inference on 2.0 Flash models
  • Strong multimodal capabilities
  • Deep Google ecosystem integration
  • Reliable and consistent output

Cons

  • Can be too cautious/filtered
  • Less personality than some models
  • Requires Google account

Verdict

The best free AI model for everyday use. Not the most creative, but reliable, fast, and surprisingly capable across modalities.
